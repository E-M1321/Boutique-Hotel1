Your Stay

EXPERIENCE THE COMFORT OF COLOR

Featuring artfully designed suites, ours is the first hotel in Europe that gives you the option of booking your room not only by bed, but also by experiencing the emotion of all 5 senses. Each room is decorated around the psychology of color, meant to make you feel exactly what you want to at the time of your stay.

On top of color, your room offers a collection of curated music playlists, incense, a handcrafted menu and an array of textures, all to support the stimulation of your senses. After a tour of beautful Berlin or the completion of one of the many complimentary activies offered within the hotel, return to your room to submerge yourself into the feelings of rest and relaxation.

Blue Room

Blue for Tranquility

Delight in a Zen selection of blues that create an atmosphere of tranquility. Sleep in a sea of blues that aids your emotional state of being free from agitation of mind, body or spirit. The perfect room for relaxation and peace of mind.

Featuring an Orchestra and Ambient-sound music playlists to further relax your mind.

Enjoy fragrances such as 'Aspen Winter' and 'Fresh Linen' through incense and candles locally made.

Green Room

Green for Rejuvenation

Nurture your mind and body in a medley of greens that create the aura of rejuvenation. The color green has health benefits that can boost blood flow, improve sleep and increase levels of serotonin in the brain, all while enhancing your emotions to a state of being revitalized. The perfect room for balance and renewal.

Featuring a R&B and Soul music playlists to further help you center your life-forces and find your balance.

Enjoy fragrances such as 'Pear & Lily' and 'Eucalyptus & Spearmint' through incense and candles locally made.

Red Room

Red for Energy

Bask in a palette of reds that create an ambience of passion. Enhances human metabolism, increases respiration rate, and raises blood pressure. The perfect room for accomplishing your to-do list and creating romance.

Featuring a Latin and "Spanish-Tinge" Swing music playlists to further relax your mind.

Enjoy fragrances such as 'Moonlight Pomegranate' and 'Summer Storm' through incense and candles locally made.

Yellow Room

Yellow for Creativity

Revel in an array of yellows that create the spirit of happiness. The bright sunny color delights and emphasize fun along with a state of well-being and contentment. The perfect room for work and play.

Featuring a Funk and Jazz music playlists to further relax your mind.

Enjoy fragrances such as 'Burst of Energy' and 'Warm Vanilla and Ginger' through incense and candles locally made.

Orange Room

Orange for Ambition

Excite you mind in a combination of oranges that generates wholesome enthusiasm. Orange invokes a feeling of warm, sunny days. Get all the things done that you have been meaning to. From painting that masterpiece to typing that annual report. The perfect room for work and focus.

Featuring a Hip-Hop and Indie music playlists to further relax your mind.

Enjoy fragrances such as 'Georgia Sunrise' and 'Sweet Orange Chili Pepper' through incense and candles locally made.

Purple Room

Purple for Spirituality

Be Drawn in by cool shades of purple that cultivates the spirit and personal piety. The color purple has a variety of effects on the mind and body, including uplifting spirits, calming the mind and nerves, enhancing the sacred, creating feelings of spirituality, increasing nurturing tendencies and sensitivity, and encouraging imagination and creativity. The perfect room for meditation and reflection.

Featuring a Soft Pop and Operatic music playlists to further relax your mind.

Enjoy fragrances such as 'Lemon Lavender' and 'Blackened Amethyst' through incense and candles locally made.

Dining

EACH ROOM IS PAIRED WITH A CAREFULLY CRAFTED MENU

The table explodes with colours and vitality thanks to seasonal fruit, tomatoes, cheeses, fragrant bread and mini cakes. Every room comes with its own hand-crafted menu that matches the mood and applifies the atmosphere. Room service from early in the morning, offering breakfast delivered to your suite, to candlelight dinners, until late at night. The lounge menu offers a great variety of options, ranging from light meals, such as fresh salads, pasta and pizza, to sophisticated European and international delicacies.
